Lunch/Dinner

  • Gyoza

    • Ingredients

    • 1 pound ground sirloin (or half beef, half pork, or try mashed tofu)
    • 2 bunches green onions
    • about half small head of nappa cabbage (or skip)
    • 1 tsp. peeled, grated ginger (mince if you have no grater)
    • 2 Tbsp. soy sauce
    • 1 Tbsp. sugar
    • 1 Tbsp. rice wine (or mirin or skip it)
    • (sprinkle of toasted sesame oil)
    • 1 package of gyoza skins (rest can be frozen)

    Instructions

    1. Wash green onions (they can be sandy) and nappa.
    2. Shake off water and slice thin.
    3. Mix veggies with meat and seasonings.
    4. Heat skillet over medium heat.
    5. Wrap about a heaping tsp. of filling in each skin.
    6. Wet edges and pinch closed.
    7. Heat 1-2 tsp. oil in pan.
    8. Fry on one side until brown (5 min. or so?).
    9. Turn, then add 1/2 cup water.
    10. Cover and let steam until done and water is evaporated (5-10 min.)
    11. Or steam in a steamer.
    12. Serve with rice and a sauce made from sugar, soy sauce, and rice vinegar.

    Serves three or four people.
