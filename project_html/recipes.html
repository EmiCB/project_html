Lunch/Dinner

  • Gyoza

    • Ingredients

    • 1 pound ground sirloin (or half beef, half pork, or try mashed tofu)
    • 2 bunches green onions
    • about half small head of nappa cabbage (or skip)
    • 1 tsp. peeled, grated ginger (mince if you have no grater)
    • 2 Tbsp. soy sauce
    • 1 Tbsp. sugar
    • 1 Tbsp. rice wine (or mirin or skip it)
    • (sprinkle of toasted sesame oil)
    • 1 package of gyoza skins (rest can be frozen)

    Instructions

    1. Wash green onions (they can be sandy) and nappa.
    2. Shake off water and slice thin.
    3. Mix veggies with meat and seasonings.
    4. Heat skillet over medium heat.
    5. Wrap about a heaping tsp. of filling in each skin.
    6. Wet edges and pinch closed.
    7. Heat 1-2 tsp. oil in pan.
    8. Fry on one side until brown (5 min. or so?).
    9. Turn, then add 1/2 cup water.
    10. Cover and let steam until done and water is evaporated (5-10 min.)
    11. Or steam in a steamer.
    12. Serve with rice and a sauce made from sugar, soy sauce, and rice vinegar.

    Serves three or four people.

  • Spaghetti

    • Ingredients

    • 1 pound ground sirloin (or Italian sausage or meatballs)
    • 1 28 oz. can tomatoes
    • 1 can tomato paste
    • 1 diced onion
    • 1 clove minced garlic
    • 1/2 tsp. basil
    • 1/2 tsp. thyme
    • 1/2 tsp. oregano
    • 1/2 tsp. ground sage
    • (1/2 tsp. dill)
    • 1 tsp. sugar
    • 1 bay leaf

    Instructions

    1. (All measurements are for dried herbs. Use what you have. Use 3 times as much fresh. Only the basil needs to be added at the end.)
    2. Heat pot over medium heat.
    3. Add 2 Tbsp. olive oil.
    4. Saute onion. When soft and translucent, add garlic. Don't brown.
    5. Add whatever sliced vegetables you want (peppers, mushroom, zucchini).
    6. (When they are done, pour in 1/4 cup red wine and let the alcohol boil off.)
    7. Add the tomatoes and their juice, tomato paste, sugar, bay leaf, and herbs.
    8. Stir in 1 pound browned ground beef (or sliced, cooked Italian sausage -- drain off the fat) for those who want meat.
    9. Simmer for 30 minutes or longer.
    10. Serve with cooked spaghetti, grated Parmesan, garlic bread.

    Serves four.

Dessert

  • Chocolate Cheesecake

      • Ingredients

    • 1.5 (8 ounce) packages of cream cheese
    • 18 Oreos
    • 1 Tbsp sugar
    • 1/8 tsp cinnamon
    • 2 Tbsp melted butter
    • 3 ounces semi-sweet chocolate
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 2 eggs
    • 1/2 cup whipping cream
    • 2 Tbsp sugar
    • 1/2 tsp vanilla

    Instructions

    1. Take out 12 ounces of cream cheese and 2 eggs.
    2. Preheat oven to 375.
    3. Scrape all the filling out of the Oreos and process into crumbs.
    4. Melt 2 Tbsp of butter in the microwave (25 seconds). Process with the cookie crumbs and 1 Tbsp sugar.
    5. Press into the bottom and sides of an 8" springform pan.
    6. Bake at 375 for about 7 minutes. Turn oven down to 350.
    7. Process the cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar and vanilla and mix till smooth.
    8. Add eggs one at a time, mixing until smooth.
    9. Whip 1 cup of whipping cream wth 2 Tbsp sugar until soft peaks form.
    10. Fold half the whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture. Save the rest for serving.
    11. Pour into crust and bake 1.5 hours.
    12. Cool in the oven (turned off and door ajar), then chill thoroughly before serving (a couple of hours more).
