Mama Saffell's Spaghetti Sauce

Recipes
  • 1 pound ground sirloin (or Italian sausage or meatballs)
  • 1 28 oz. can tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp. basil
  • 1/2 tsp. thyme
  • 1/2 tsp. oregano
  • 1/2 tsp. ground sage
  • (1/2 tsp. dill)
  • 1 tsp. sugar
  • 1 bay leaf
  1. (All measurements are for dried herbs. Use what you have. Use 3 times as much fresh. Only the basil needs to be added at the end.)
  2. Heat pot over medium heat.
  3. Add 2 Tbsp. olive oil.
  4. Saute onion. When soft and translucent, add garlic. Don't brown.
  5. Add whatever sliced vegetables you want (peppers, mushroom, zucchini).
  6. (When they are done, pour in 1/4 cup red wine and let the alcohol boil off.)
  7. Add the tomatoes and their juice, tomato paste, sugar, bay leaf, and herbs.
  8. Stir in 1 pound browned ground beef (or sliced, cooked Italian sausage -- drain off the fat) for those who want meat.
  9. Simmer for 30 minutes or longer.
  10. Serve with cooked spaghetti, grated Parmesan, garlic bread.

Serves four.
